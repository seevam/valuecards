Values Card Sort Activity

Discover what matters most to you by sorting these values into three categories. This will help connect your personality to your career aspirations.

How to Use This Activity

  • Step 1: Read each value card carefully and think about how important it is to you personally
  • Step 2: Drag and drop each card into one of the three categories on the right
  • Step 3: Be honest - there are no right or wrong answers, only what's true for you
  • Step 4: Review your sorted values and discuss patterns with your mentor
